About Shayne Rushton

Welcome to my website and please call me Shayne. (Though my name is Matthew), I go by my middle name. Blame my mother. It was either going to be Shayne or Shayna. Long story, but I like my name just the same.

I would like to take a minute to talk about myself and my interests. I am currently in school to learn Web Design and Web App Development. I have an amateur background in this field, which has helped me so far by allowing me to understand the content much better than if I had just started learning. I love the technology field and am really looking forward to being able to incorporate this discipline into my career going forward.

I am a father of 2 boys. I am a musician. I also enjoy working with wood to build things. I have built a t-shirt press for a six color print and currently I am in the process of building a guitar. I spend as much time and energy as I can get on my project because I want to master the art of making guitars. I think the toughest part is the painting, but mistakes have been made on each step of the way. No pain, no gain. I hope to one day play one of these guitars.

I am here at Edmonds Community College because I found out about a retraining program. I had to do a lot of foot work and I needed a lot of help along the way, but thanks to the knowledgeable and helpful staff in the office, I was able to start my first degree ever. It has been amazing so far. Look around my site and explore some of my interests and be sure to sign my guest book.
